About Escapade

Escapade has been the premium events bar operator in the North West and further afield for over a decade. We create unforgettable memories for guests through our drinks and service providing all the necessary elements required to curate a seamless event experience, including a wide selection of cocktails, refreshments, staffing, and end-to-end solutions.

From world-class venue operation to exclusive cocktail parties for any occasion, our team have the creativity and expertise to make your dream occasion a reality. Whether it’s vodka from the sprinklers or a glass of sherry for your Grandma, it will be delivered with the Escapade touch.
